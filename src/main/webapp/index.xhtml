P4PPP Digital Solutions is a directory of digital health solutions utilised for management of P4PPP19 pandemic, giving wider visibility for possible re-use and support. This initiative was triggered by the catastrophic effects of the P4PPP19 pandemic and the realisation of how digital health solutions could assist to overcome the global health challenges emerged due to overwhelming healthcare needs. It was further observed that there is a lack of coordination and support for digital health solution development, deployment and sustenance, at a global scale. Hence, the need for this global platform - P4PPP Digital Solutions
