About Us

Who we are:

The Platform for Planet Place and People (P4PPP) is the first virtual hub to be established by the Commonwealth Centre for Digital Health, with a vision of the CWCDH is to strategically digitalise the health systems across the components identified in the Commonwealthâ€™s Systems Framework for Healthy Policy (SFHP) to build sustainable health systems that act as a common good for all. SFHP seeks to advance global health security and sustainable well-being for all through health system strengthening for implementation of Universal health coverage, supporting economic growth and SGDs, providing a framework for health systems assessment, planning, training, evaluation and accreditation and strengthening leadership and governance.






Goals

Create and maintain a platform to host re-usable digital health solutions for management of epidemics and pandemics

Have global visibility and scope in the take-up and use of suitable digital health solutions

Share experiences, identify and promote success stories and learn from failures

Create partnerships to support for digital health projects and implementations at national, regional and global scale

Promote open source digital health solutions

Promote open data and related research
